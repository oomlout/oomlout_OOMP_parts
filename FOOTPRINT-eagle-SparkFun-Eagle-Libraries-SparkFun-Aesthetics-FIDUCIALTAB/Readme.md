- description: &lt;h3&gt;Fiducial Panel Tab&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This is half of a tab, with fiducial reference mark, used to connect individual boards in a panel of PCBs.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Two of these half tabs, rotated in opposite directions, can be connected together to form a full tab, with two fiducial markings. Place along the edge of a panel of PCBs, this will allow for panels with boards not connected by v-scores, which allows for easier depanelization where there are overhanging components. It can also be used for designs too small to accomodate fiducial marks on individual boards.&lt;/p&gt;
